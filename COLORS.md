# Colors

## Twente Red

Twente Red is the official and primary color of the Twente.me project. It's inspired by the color of the region of Twente, as showcased by the red in the logos for FC Twente and Twente Branding.

- Hex: #F00000
- RGB: (240, 0, 0)
- CMYK: (0, 100, 100, 6)
